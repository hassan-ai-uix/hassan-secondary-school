About Us

Who we are & what we stand for

Our Story

Hassan Secondary School is a community-focused institution in Mukono District committed to academic excellence and holistic development. We believe every learner can thrive with the right guidance and opportunities.

Mission

To deliver quality education that empowers students with knowledge, values, and skills to succeed in a changing world.

Vision

To be a leading center of learning in Uganda, known for integrity, innovation, and outstanding student outcomes.

Academics

Balanced curriculum for growth and excellence

Sciences

Chemistry, Biology, Physics, and Mathematics with well-equipped labs for practical learning.

STEM Focus

Arts & Humanities

History, Geography, CRE/IRE, Literature, and Languages to build critical thinking and communication.

Well-rounded

Co-curricular

Music, Dance & Drama, Debate, ICT Club, Football, Netball and more for talent development.
